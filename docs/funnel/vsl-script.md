## FILMING NOTES

- **Setup:** one locked shot, chest-up, clean background (navy/dark wall or office). Wear plain dark clothing — no logos, no patterns. Key light warm (gold side), fill low.
- **Delivery:** slow down 20% from what feels natural. Pause one beat at every paragraph break — those are your edit points.
- **Safety takes:** read the HOOK 3 different ways (statement, question, cold-open mid-sentence). The hook decides everything.
- **Look:** straight into the lens the entire time. If using a teleprompter, put it directly over the lens.
