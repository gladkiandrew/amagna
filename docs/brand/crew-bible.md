## House style (LOCKED 2026-06-15: polished 3D animation)
Target: **premium 3D-animated film** (Pixar / DreamWorks grade) — clean, fierce, classy, royal.
All five share this 3D look; text removed from all. (Mansa + Vela are new characters replacing Hero + Thales.)
Cinematic key light, soft rim, believable materials (brass, leather, wool, wood). NOT photoreal,
NOT flat cartoon, NOT painterly-sketch. Palette on characters: navy/deep-purple bases, antique-gold
and brass accents, royal-purple cloth, cream highlights.

> ⚠ The 6 existing portraits are split: **Zeno, Solon, Thales** read as polished 3D; **Exodus, Hero**
> read as painterly fantasy. They also have text baked in. Before registering Elements we regenerate
> ONE clean, text-free, unified-3D canonical portrait per character (using the existing portrait as the
> likeness reference) so the cast matches.

## THE SHIP — the Amagna flagship
**Higgsfield Element ID (prop):** `1cff516e-9ee5-4be6-80f3-02b908698a20`
A mythic Norse dragon-longship. Carved **gold dragon-head prow** and a curled dragon-tail stern,
warm honey-wood clinker hull, a single tall mast. **Square sail striped royal-purple and cream bearing
the gold "M" monogram.** A row of round wooden shields along the hull, each rimmed in brass and bearing
a small gold crew sigil. Premium, heroic, heritage. This is the brand's vessel — it appears in nearly
every brand piece.

## THE WORLD — the Voyage
A vast dark ocean under a deep royal-purple sky that fades to an antique-gold glow at the horizon.
Gold light glints scattered on near-black water; soft mist; aged-maritime-map atmosphere. Calm, epic,
expensive. Coastal towns / golden harbours appear as destinations (home services rooftops, real-estate
estates) rendered in the same heritage gold.
