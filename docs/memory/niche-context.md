# Niche Context — Amagna AI

> Reference for niche positioning across the site, funnels, and agent tuning. All five niches below are
> **active and equal** — there is no single "lead" niche. Each has (or will have) a dedicated funnel,
> ad creative, voice, and agent tuning. Never mix assets across niches.

## Positioning rule

The core offering — **Autonomous Marketing Systems** — serves any operator who wants their marketing to
run itself. Site-wide copy (homepage, Who We Serve, Our Story) reflects this breadth. The five niches
below get dedicated funnels; positioning must never read as "we only do [one niche]."

## Active niches (all equal — no lead/secondary)

1. **Home Services** — HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping. Pitch: predictable, owned
   leads instead of feast-or-famine seasons and rented lead-reseller calls. ICP: owner-operator,
   $500K–$3M revenue, mid-sized Midwest metro.

2. **Real Estate Agents + Owners** — Solo agents, teams, brokers, owners. Pitch: stay top of mind 24/7
   so the sphere stays warm and listings keep coming. ICP: solo or small team, $250K–$1.5M GCI,
   listing-focused (not buyer leads).

3. **Medical Offices** — Private practices, dental, med-spa, specialty clinics. Pitch: automated,
   compliant patient acquisition with reviews, reminders, and recall. A human approves anything
   patient-facing.

4. **Ecommerce Brands** — DTC and ecommerce on Shopify and beyond. Pitch: an always-on creative engine
   plus managed paid acquisition and email/SMS flows to acquire customers and bring them back.

5. **Multi-Location Businesses** — Multi-location operators, franchises, and groups. Pitch: one central
   brain that keeps every location on-brand with local relevance, plus a roll-up report.

## Not in current GTM

- **Political Candidates** — long-term roadmap only. NOT part of the current go-to-market. Do not
  generate political-niche assets or list it as an active niche unless Andrew explicitly asks.

## Funnel + asset rule

Each niche gets its own funnel, ad creative, voice, and agent tuning. Use the matching playbook in
`docs/playbooks/` once created. Never mix niche assets. Site copy stays broad — no single niche
dominates the homepage or Who We Serve.
