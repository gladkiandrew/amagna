## The plan (as generated)

### Headline
Turn HydroClean from a word-of-mouth shop into the go-to pressure washing crew in Saginaw — with a calendar that fills itself and a commercial pipeline running in the background.

### Summary
Right now your revenue swings month to month because it's tied to door hangers, referrals, and whatever organic posts hit. You've got the channels (GBP, Meta, LinkedIn) but they're not doing real work for you yet. Over the next ~90 days we lock down Saginaw's local search so homeowners find you first, turn your before/afters into ads that actually book jobs, and start a steady LinkedIn + walk-in commercial outreach motion aimed at property managers, HOAs, and small commercial sites. By day 90 you should have a predictable flow of residential bookings hitting your calendar without you chasing, plus 3–8 real commercial conversations in motion.

### Phase 1 — Get found and get booked (residential foundation) · First 2 weeks
- Overhaul your Google Business Profile: add 'Pressure Washing Service' as primary category, load 30+ before/after photos with Saginaw geo-tags, write service-specific descriptions for house washing, driveway, roof, gutter, concrete, and turn on messaging + booking link.
- Launch a review push — text every customer from the last 12 months a one-tap Google review link, target 25+ new reviews in 30 days. Most operators in your spot see GBP calls jump within weeks of crossing the 50-review mark.
- Add an instant-book / instant-quote form to your website (or a simple Calendly + quote-request page) so homeowners can self-schedule estimates 24/7 instead of waiting on a callback.
- Set up an automatic follow-up sequence: every inbound lead gets a text within 2 minutes, a follow-up at 24 hours, and a 'still want this on the calendar?' nudge at day 3.

### Phase 2 — Turn on paid demand + content engine · Weeks 3–6
- Launch a small Meta ads test ($30–50/day) targeting homeowners in the highest-value Saginaw zip codes (Thomas Township, Saginaw Township, Frankenmuth, Freeland) with a 'House Wash Special' or 'Driveway + Concrete Combo' offer — lead form goes straight to your calendar.
- Film 2–3 short videos a week on the job: dirty-to-clean reveals, soft-wash on siding, oxidation removal, satisfied homeowner reactions. Post to Instagram Reels, Facebook, and a YouTube Shorts channel — these double as ad creative.
- Start a Google Local Services Ads (LSA) profile and a small Google Search budget on 'pressure washing Saginaw,' 'house washing near me,' 'roof cleaning Saginaw' — high-intent, ready-to-book traffic.
- Stand up a simple branded landing page per service (house wash, roof, concrete, commercial) so ads and GBP send people to the right offer instead of a generic homepage.

### Phase 3 — Open the commercial pipeline · Weeks 6–12
- Build a target list of 150–250 Saginaw-area commercial accounts: property management companies, HOAs, apartment complexes, real estate brokerages (for listing prep washes), restaurants/QSRs (drive-thrus, dumpster pads), churches, medical offices, and storefronts on Bay Rd and Tittabawassee.
- Run a LinkedIn outreach motion from Andrew's profile: connect with property managers, facility managers, and commercial real estate agents in Saginaw/Bay City/Midland with a short, no-pitch intro + a before/after carousel post weekly so you stay visible in their feed.
- Pair the digital outreach with in-person drops — branded folder with a one-page capabilities sheet, photos, COI, and a 'free 15-minute walk-and-quote' offer hand-delivered to 5–10 commercial prospects per week.
- Build a recurring-maintenance offer (quarterly storefront wash, monthly dumpster pad, annual building wash) with simple flat pricing so commercial decision-makers can say yes in one meeting.

### What the crew handles for you
- Keeping your Google Business Profile fed weekly with photos, posts, and service updates so you stay at the top of Saginaw local search
- Running your Meta and Google ads — writing copy, cutting your job-site footage into ads, swapping creative, and watching cost-per-booked-job
- Cutting your raw before/after phone footage into short-form videos for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts every week
- Handling the 2-minute lead response, follow-up texts, review requests, and rebooking reminders so no lead or past customer falls through the cracks
- Running the LinkedIn + email + door-drop commercial outreach to property managers and local businesses, and putting booked walk-throughs on Andrew's calendar
